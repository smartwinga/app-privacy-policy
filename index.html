Privacy Policy

for WingaSmart

Welcome to WingaSmart! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect through our mobile application and how it is used and safeguarded.

Types of Data Collected:

  • Personal Data: While using our app, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. This may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, and address.

This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our app that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

We may disclose your personal data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

The security of your data is important to us. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
